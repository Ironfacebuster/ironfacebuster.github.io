I wouldn't call myself the world's best photographer, but I do think I can snap some beautiful pictures. As someone just getting into a craft, my tools are somewhat limited. My main shooter is a 2000-something Canon Rebel XT; a hand-me-down from my mom! I recently picked up a slightly older Canon Rebel X, a 35mm film camera, as well. My final shooter is something of an everyday carry: my phone! I have a Google Pixel 5, which (in certain conditions) takes absolutely breathtaking pictures. This humble set of tools is no Fujifilm GFX100S, but you make the most of what you have, you know?

Regardless, I'm glad to be able to share my work with you. I hope you enjoy my photos as much as I did when I took them. Please enjoy. 2021
